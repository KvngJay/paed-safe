PaedSafe — Important Notice
PaedSafe is a calculation aid for qualified anaesthesia providers.

It is not a certified medical device. It does not replace clinical training, institutional protocols, or your own judgement.

Every dose calculated by this app must be verified by you before administration. You are responsible for confirming:
  • The patient's weight and age are correct
  • The calculated dose is appropriate for this patient's clinical condition
  • The dose aligns with your institution's formulary and protocols

Formula sources and version numbers are shown on every result screen.

By continuing, you confirm that you are a qualified healthcare provider and that you will independently verify all outputs before use.
