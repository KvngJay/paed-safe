PaedSafe — Important Notice
PaedSafe is a calculation aid for qualified anaesthesia providers.

It is not a certified medical device. It does not replace clinical training, institutional protocols, or your judgement.

Every dose calculated by this app must be verified before administration. You are responsible for confirming:
  • The patient's weight and age are correct
  • The calculated dose is appropriate for this patient's clinical condition
  • The dose aligns with your institution's formulary and protocols

Formula sources and version numbers are shown on every result.

By continuing, you confirm that you are a qualified healthcare provider and will independently verify all outputs before use.
